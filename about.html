Introduction

Our Commitment To Our People is The Key To Success

Megha Travels, a unique travel group was founded by Sandhya Gudipudi in 2013, and is synonymous throughout India for coach tours. Megha Travels is Recognized by the Government of Telangana and Karnataka, Dept. of Tourism and accepted by all Nationalized and Non-nationalized Institutions for availing LTC/LFC facility. Our network of branches & agents are spread all over Andhra, Karnataka & neighboring States, and fleet owners of Twenty coaches. Megha Travels specializes in handling special groups, leisure groups, Honey-Moon Packages, family groups, School groups and catering to any customized or tailor made programmes.
